Taking Care of Your Goods

Cookie Care

Storing

Store in air-tight container for up to 1 week

Fridge storage allowed

Freezing

Freeze cookies for longer storage

Re-Heating

Best method: Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and warm the cookies (out of the packaging) for 4-5 minutes (7-10 minutes if frozen)

Microwave: Heat out of the packaging for 8-10 seconds

Sourdough Bread Care

Storing

Store in air-tight container for up to 5 week

Fridge storage is not adviced

Freezing

Freeze bread in air-tight container for up to 2 months

Re-Heating

Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and warm the bread for 2-3 minutes

If frozen, let bread thaw out at room temperature, then preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and warm the bread for 5-7 minutes
